General questions

How to started with bitClassroom?

Classroom is available to anyone with a bitClassroom account. Your administrator only can sign up here. Select whether you are a teacher or a student, then create or join a class. If you are the administrator, you can find more information about turning the service on and off in Access to bitClassroom.

Who is eligible for bitClassroom?

BitClassroom is available to all schools for Education

Do I need Gmail to use bitClassroom?

You do not need to have Gmail enabled for your account to use bitClassroom; however, if you don't have Gmail enabled, you will not receive email notifications. Note that if you have your own mail server set up and receive Google Drive notifications, you will also receive bitClassroom notifications.

Is there a bitClassroom app I can use on my mobile device or tablet?

Yes, the bitClassroom app is available for iOS and Android devices.
